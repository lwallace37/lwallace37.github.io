UUV Prototyping of an Unmanned Underwater Vehicle

Project Description

The final project for my Engineering Design class (MAE 321) was to design, manufacture, and test a size-limited Unmanned Underwater Vehicle (UUV) to accomplish certain performance-based metrics. With a large team of 12, we spent 4 weeks learning the ins and outs of rapid prototyping.

Project Details

  • PTC Creo 2.0 to model and analyze the UUV
  • Team of 12 broken into 4 main sub-teams: buoyancy, propulsion, frame, and waterproofing
  • General Design: 2 PVC pipes to house electronics, Central plate to hold verticle thrusters
  • 4 motors, 4 ESCs, 2 batteries and a controller system (Arduino)
