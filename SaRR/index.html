SaRR Prototyping of a Semi-Autonomous Search and Rescue Robot

Project Description

The final project for my Engineering Design (MAE 322) course was to design and construct a Search and Rescue Robot (SaRR) with the purpose of retrieving and delivering a medical kit. With a team of seven, we designed our robot to lift and hold a "medical kit", autonomously navigate a chute, and autonomously find a light source.

Project Details

  • Use PTC Creo 2.0 to design and analyze parts.
  • Use light and proximity sensors for autonomous navigation
  • Use a Mini Ardiuno for to control motors.
